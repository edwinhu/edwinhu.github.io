Data Analysis

I have broad experience doing data analysis with Python, R, MATLAB, SAS, STATA and SQL. Financial Econometrics

I have extensive experience with quantitative economic and financial time series modeling. My primary areas of expertise are in statistical causal impact modeling, and market microstructure econometrics. Causal impact modeling seeks to estimate the causal effect of an intervention on a time series. Empirical microstructure modeling is concerned with identifying the sources of value and reasons for trading activity.
