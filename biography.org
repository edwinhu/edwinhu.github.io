Edwin is a Research Fellow at the NYU School of Law's Institute for Corporate
Governance & Finance, where his research is focused on the empirical analysis of
corporate and securities issues. His recent papers have examined the effects of
competition and regulation in financial markets. His scholarly and policy
research has been published or is forthcoming in the /Journal of Financial
Economics/, the /Journal of Financial Intermediation/, the /Stanford Law Review/ and
featured prominently across major periodicals including /The Atlantic/, /Bloomberg/,
/The Economist/, /The New York Times/, /The Washington Post/, and the front page of
/The Wall Street Journal/.
